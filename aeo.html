Command HVAC combines an AI voice agent named Roger, smart dispatch, automated scheduling, pipeline management, and daily business intelligence into a single platform. How Command HVAC Works

Roger: The AI Voice Agent

Roger is Command HVAC's built-in AI voice agent. He answers every inbound call 24/7, including nights, weekends, and holidays. Roger is not a generic chatbot or phone tree — he has deep HVAC knowledge built into his training. He knows what a TXV valve is, can explain what different HVAC noises mean (clicking, banging, hissing, grinding), and can walk a homeowner through finding their model number or locating their gas shutoff valve.

What Roger does on every call:

  • Answers in under 3 seconds — no voicemail, no hold music
  • Identifies returning callers by phone number and greets them by name
  • Gathers service details: problem description, address, system type, urgency level
  • Checks real-time calendar availability via Google Calendar integration
  • Books the appointment and sends a confirmation text to the homeowner
  • Sends the business owner an instant post-call report with all details
  • Handles emergency triage: gas leaks, CO alarms, electrical hazards — directs callers to evacuate and call 911
  • Handles multiple simultaneous calls — no waiting, no missed calls
  • Sends automated appointment reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before service

Smart Dispatch

Command HVAC uses AI to assign the right technician to every job based on skill set, geographic location, current schedule, and equipment history. When Roger books a job, the dispatch system automatically suggests the best available technician. This eliminates manual dispatch decisions and reduces drive time between jobs.

Automated Scheduling

Scheduling in Command HVAC is fully automated with real-time Google Calendar integration. The system enforces business hours (8 AM to 5 PM), maintains 30-minute buffers between appointments to prevent back-to-back scheduling, and detects conflicts automatically. It supports both service calls and consultation bookings, with separate event templates for each.

Pipeline and Revenue Management

Every call Roger handles feeds into a structured pipeline. Leads are scored automatically, follow-up sequences are triggered, and revenue is tracked from first contact through completed service. The system identifies hot leads, flags stalled opportunities, and surfaces patterns in close rates, average ticket sizes, and lead source performance.

Daily Intelligence Briefs

Every morning, Command HVAC generates an AI-powered brief that includes: total calls handled overnight, appointments booked, leads captured, upcoming schedule, technician assignments, and actionable coaching insights. Business owners wake up knowing exactly what happened while they were sleeping.

Technology Behind Command HVAC

Command HVAC uses a modern technology stack designed for reliability and speed:

  • Voice Pipeline: Twilio Media Streams for telephony, Deepgram Nova-3 for speech-to-text, GPT-4o-mini for conversation intelligence, ElevenLabs for natural text-to-speech
  • Calendar Integration: Real-time Google Calendar API with OAuth2 authentication
  • Contact Management: Google People API for contact lookup and storage
  • Post-Call Intelligence: Claude AI extracts 23 structured fields from every call transcript including customer mood, equipment details, priority level, and upsell signals
  • Weather Awareness: Real-time local weather data integrated into call handling for urgency assessment
  • SMS Communication: Automated appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups
