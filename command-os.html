What It Does

EVERY PART OF YOUR
BUSINESS. COVERED.

Not a checklist of features. A picture of what actually changes when Command OS is running your business.

📞
Never Miss a Lead Again
Every call answered by Roger, 24/7. Missed calls get an instant text-back. Web forms get an immediate response. Social DMs get captured and qualified. Every lead source — covered.
🗓
Dispatch That Runs Itself
7-step guided dispatch intake. Smart job assignment based on certifications, location, and workload. On-call rotation tracked automatically. Your dispatcher works smarter. Your operation doesn't collapse when they're out.
🔧
Techs Who Show Up Prepared
Full customer history, equipment records, previous service notes, and AI pre-diagnostic support on every tech's phone before they arrive. Better prepared techs close bigger tickets and handle fewer escalations.
📊
Real Numbers, Real Time
Live pipeline value, dispatch rate, average ticket, tech performance — visible from your phone at any moment. No more waiting 30 days to find out how the month went. You see it as it happens.
🧠
Follow-Up That Never Stops
Automated follow-up sequences run until the deal closes or the lead says stop. Review requests, re-engagement, no-show recovery, seasonal campaigns — all running in the background without anyone touching them.
📋
Coaching Data for Every Manager
Manager Coaching Insights show exactly where each tech is strong, where they're leaving money, and what kind of coaching will actually move the needle. Turn managers into leaders, not just schedulers.
⭐
Reputation on Autopilot
Review requests go out automatically 10 minutes after every job close. Negative feedback gets flagged internally before it goes public. Your online reputation builds while your team is in the field.
📈
Weekly Intelligence Reports
Every Monday morning, SecondBrain writes your full business report. Revenue, close rate, top performer, pipeline forecast, action items. You start every week knowing exactly where you stand.
🏦
Built for What It's Worth
A business running on documented systems, consistent processes, and clean data is worth multiples more at exit than one running on the owner. We build that infrastructure into the foundation from day one.
