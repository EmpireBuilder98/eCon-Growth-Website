The Origin

TEN YEARS.
EVERY ROLE.
ONE VISION.

Kristopher Cravens didn't build Command OS in a boardroom. He built it after a decade in the field — sweating through installs, running service calls, dispatching techs, closing sales, and at times running entire HVAC companies solo while the owner was gone for weeks at a time.

He saw the same problems everywhere he worked. Good operators. Great technicians. Terrible systems. Businesses running entirely on whoever had the most information in their head. Revenue leaking out the back door through missed calls. Owners drowning in the middle of operations they built to set themselves free.

He didn't write a business plan. He built what he wished he had. What every employer he ever worked for wished they had. And then he found the right partner to help him take it to an industry that desperately needs it.
